The Vision

What if your growth wasn't a checklist —
but a solar system?

Most productivity tools treat you like a machine — metrics, streaks, notifications. SurjyoPath says: you are not a machine. You are a solar system.

At your center burns a sun — the truest version of yourself. Around it, six planets orbit, each with its own gravity, its own rhythm: Journal, Goals, Library, Publications, Friends, and Profile.

The name comes from Bengali "Surjyo" (সূর্য — sun) and "Path" (পথ — way/path). The Sun's Way.

Powered By AMD

The AI Intelligence Layer

Every AI interaction in SurjyoPath runs through Fireworks AI on AMD MI300X GPUs, hosted via AMD Dev Cloud. Three specialized engines power the experience:

Knock
Journal Engine

Analyzes journal entries for hidden patterns, cognitive distortions, and growth opportunities. Surfaces questions, not answers.

Pathway
Goal Engine

Transforms ambitions into compass-aligned learning pathways with structured phases, each with poetic titles and actionable descriptions.

Polish
Publication Engine

Elevates raw thoughts into radiant pieces while preserving the author's unique voice. Side-by-side comparison for full control.

Behind the Cosmos

Additional Information

🌌 The Philosophy

Personal growth is not linear — it's orbital. You don't progress in a straight line from A to B. You orbit yourself, returning to the same themes at higher altitudes, deeper understanding, brighter light.

🌏 The Name

From Bengali "Surjyo" (সূর্য — sun) and "Path" (পথ — way/path). It means "The Sun's Way." Every user is a sun. The app is their solar system.

♿ Accessibility

Reduced-motion preferences respected, keyboard-navigable planets, ARIA labels on every interactive element, and 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratios throughout.

🎨 The Galaxy as Interface

The galaxy visualization is not decoration — it's the interface. You navigate by looking at the planets. The central sun pulses with your engagement. Planets move at different speeds because different parts of your life grow at different rhythms.

📚 Original Content

Four original books written exclusively for SurjyoPath — The Sunrise Principle, Wayfinding, Roots & Radiance, and The Art of Inner Weather — each with six complete chapters of reflective prose. ~46,000 characters of original content.

⚡ AMD Silicon

Every AI interaction — every thought knock, every goal pathway, every publication polish — runs through Fireworks AI on AMD MI300X GPUs via AMD Dev Cloud, alongside AI/ML API and AI21Lab. The galaxy you see is powered by the silicon you don't see.
