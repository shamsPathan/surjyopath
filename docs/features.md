# 🌌 Features Overview

Every feature in SurjyoPath is a **planet** in your personal solar system. Each one orbits around **you** — the Sun.

---

## 🌞 Galaxy Dashboard

Your cosmic home. An interactive solar system where you navigate the universe.

- **Animated orbits** — Six planets rotate around the central sun at their own pace
- **Pulsating corona** — The sun radiates with layered glow effects
- **Hover to discover** — Each planet reveals its name on hover
- **Click to travel** — Navigate to any module with a single click
- **Ambient starfield** — A living, breathing backdrop
- **Light/Dark mode** — Toggle between cosmic dark and ethereal light themes

---

## 📖 AI-Powered Journal

Your thoughts, illuminated. Write freely, and let Knock AI find the patterns you might have missed.

### How it works

1. **Write** — Compose a journal entry with a title and your thoughts
2. **Knock** — Click the "Knock" button to invoke the AI analysis
3. **Discover** — Knock AI returns:
   - 🧩 **Hidden patterns** — Recurring themes you might not notice
   - ❓ **Reflective questions** — Questions that deepen your understanding
   - 👁️ **Blind spots** — Perspectives you haven't considered
   - 🎯 **Learning opportunities** — Ideas to explore further

### Features

- Rich text composition
- Auto-categorization of entries
- Thought timeline with mood patterns
- Tag-based filtering and search
- Rate-limited AI calls (fair usage for everyone)

> *"What you wrote about resilience three times this month… what's that telling you?"*

## 🎯 Adaptive Learning Pathways

Turn any ambition into a structured learning journey.

### How it works

1. **Define** — Set a goal: "Learn Python" or "Become a better writer"
2. **Pathway** — AI generates a complete curriculum with modules and chapters
3. **Progress** — Work through each chapter, track your completion
4. **Assess** — Take quizzes to reinforce your understanding

### Features

- Auto-generated course structures
- Milestone-based progression
- Built-in assessments and quizzes
- Progress tracking with visual indicators
- Tags and categorization
- Dynamic adaptation based on your pace

A beautiful reading companion that respects the depth of your learning.

## ✍️ AI-Assisted Publications

Your voice matters. Polish AI refines your writing without erasing *you*.

### How it works

1. **Draft** — Write your article or essay
2. **Polish** — Invoke Polish AI to review and refine
3. **Review** — See suggested improvements while keeping your voice
4. **Publish** — Share with the community

### Polish AI Returns

- ✨ **Polished version** — Improved clarity and flow
- 📝 **Summary** — A concise overview of your article
- 💡 **Improvements** — Specific suggestions for enhancement

### Social Features

- Public articles with beautiful typography
- Comment system for community discussion
- Like and share functionality
- Author profiles with publication history

## 🎨 Design System

SurjyoPath features a carefully crafted design system:

- **Typography** — Inter (UI) + Space Grotesk (headings) + Crimson Pro (reading)
- **Colors** — OKLCH color space for vibrant, perceptually-uniform colors
- **Dark/Light modes** — Two complete themes with smooth transitions
- **Custom scrollbars** — Subtle, integrated design
- **Glow effects** — Primary and accent glow tokens
- **Dot grid backgrounds** — Subtle texture on surfaces

---

## 🔐 Privacy & Security

- All journal entries are **private by default**
- Publications can be shared **at your discretion**
- **JWT-based authentication** via Supabase
- **Protected routes** ensure data stays with its owner
- **Rate limiting** protects against abuse
